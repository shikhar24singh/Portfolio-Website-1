Mommy'sMade

Crafting a warm, premium identity for a homemade bakery brand

Role

Graphic Designer & Photographer

Timeline

40 Weeks

Tools

Illustrator, Photoshop, Canva, Lightroom, Affinity Designer

Deliverables

Logo, Posters, Social, Photography, Recipe Book

The Challenge

Transform a passion project into a cohesive, market-ready brand.

Goal

Build a premium homemade bakery identity that feels authentic, warm, and elevated. The brand needed to stand out in a crowded market while staying true to its "crafted at home" roots.

Approach

Warm, elegant, minimal design with earthy tones, refined typography, and intentional whitespace. Every visual element was crafted to evoke comfort, quality, and premium homemade care.

Outcome

Strong and cohesive brand presence across all touchpoints. Increased customer trust, social media engagement, and a visual system that scales seamlessly for future product lines.
