Event Coverage & Visual Identity

Photography & Media Club

Capturing campus energy, designing narratives, and building a cohesive visual ecosystem for student events

Role

Photographer & Lead Graphic Designer

Timeline

2 Academic Years

Tools

Photoshop, Lightroom, Illustrator, Canva, Affinity Designer, DaVinci Resolve

Deliverables

Event Coverage, Poster Series, Social Campaigns, Brand Guidelines

The Challenge

Fast-paced college events demand rapid documentation and consistent promotional content. The club needed a unified visual language that scaled across fests, workshops, and digital platforms.

Goal

Cover major college events with professional-grade photography while designing promotional materials that maintain brand consistency, boost engagement, and attract student participation.

Approach

Implemented a streamlined post-event workflow for rapid editing. Created modular poster templates, established a consistent color grading system, and maintained a structured social media grid for all campaigns.

Outcome

40% increase in event attendance, highly engaged online community, and a reusable visual system that empowered the club to scale content production without sacrificing quality or consistency.

Visual Deliverables

Live Event Coverage

Dynamic stage photography, crowd moments, and behind-the-scenes documentation

Social Media Creatives

Grid-aligned templates, story highlights, and promotional carousels

Workshops & Collaborations

Documenting knowledge-sharing sessions, team setups, and interactive learning environments

Annual Fest Coverage

End-to-end visual documentation from pre-event promos to live highlights and post-event recaps

"Photography captures the moment. Design gives it a voice. Together, they build community."
— Guiding principle for all club campaigns
